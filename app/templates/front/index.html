Our Story

關於我們 About Us

Hualien Kitchen was born from a simple love of honest cooking. Nestled on Taiwan's wild east coast, Hualien has always been a place apart — dramatic gorges, open Pacific skies, and communities that have fed their families with care for generations.

花蓮廚房源自對誠實料理的熱愛。坐落於台灣壯麗的東海岸， 花蓮自古以來是一片獨特的土地——壯闊的峽谷、遼闊的太平洋 天際線，以及數代人用心烹飪、滋養家庭的深厚傳統。

Every dish we serve carries that spirit: simple ingredients, prepared with intention, served with warmth.
